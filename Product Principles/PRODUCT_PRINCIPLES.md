# Jobhunter Product Principles

## Mission

Help job seekers make informed application decisions before investing hours tailoring their applications.

---

## Product Promise

**Know Before You Apply.**

---

## Brand Promise

**No ads. No gimmicks. Just better application decisions.**

---

## What We Believe

Job searching is already hard.

People shouldn't have to guess whether an opportunity is worth their time.

AI shouldn't replace human judgment.

It should provide better information so people can make better decisions.

---

## Our Values

### Respect the user's time.

Every interaction should save time or provide meaningful value.

---

### Build trust through transparency.

Explain why a recommendation was made.

Never hide the reasoning.

---

### Collaborate instead of dictate.

The final decision always belongs to the user.

---

### Keep the experience effortless.

Don't make people learn the product.

Let them experience it.

---

### Reduce friction.

Every screen should make the next step obvious.

---

## Homepage Filter

Before adding anything to the homepage, ask:

> Does this help someone decide to click "Analyze My Fit?"

If not, it probably belongs somewhere else.

---

## Feature Filter

Before building a feature, ask:

> Does this help someone make a better application decision?

If not, it probably isn't MVP.

---

## Success

A successful user leaves thinking:

> "That was valuable and gave me good insight. I'll use it again."

---

## Long-Term Vision

Jobhunter isn't trying to tell people what to do.

It's helping them think more clearly before investing their time.
